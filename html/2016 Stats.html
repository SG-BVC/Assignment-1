2016 Season

In 2016, Dak Prescott had an impressive rookie season as the quarterback for the Dallas Cowboys. He started all 16 games and helped lead the team to a 13-3 record. Prescott threw for 3,667 yards and 23 touchdowns, with only four interceptions. He showed great skill and poise on the field, earning him the NFL Offensive Rookie of the Year award. His performance, along with that of running back Ezekiel Elliott, played a big role in the Cowboys' success that year.
