2021 Season

In 2021, Dak Prescott made a strong comeback after his injury the previous year. He played in all 17 games for the Dallas Cowboys and threw for 4,449 yards, with 37 touchdown passes and 10 interceptions. Prescott helped lead the Cowboys to a successful season, finishing with a 12-5 record and winning the NFC East division title. He played well in big moments, earning Pro Bowl honors for his performance. Despite their success, the Cowboys lost in the first round of the playoffs, but Prescott's strong play showed he was back to being one of the top quarterbacks in the NFL.
