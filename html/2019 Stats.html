2019 Season

In 2019, Dak Prescott had one of his best seasons as the quarterback for the Dallas Cowboys. He started all 16 games and threw for 4,902 yards, which was a career high. Prescott also recorded 30 touchdown passes and had 11 interceptions. He led the Cowboys to an 8-8 record, just missing the playoffs. During the season, he set several team records and showed his ability to make big plays. Prescott's strong performance helped him gain recognition as one of the top quarterbacks in the NFL.
