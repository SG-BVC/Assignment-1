Dak Prescott

Dak Prescott first started playing football in high school at Haughton High in Louisiana, where he played quarterback and led his team to a district championship. He then attended Mississippi State University, where he became the starting quarterback in 2013. Prescott gained national attention in 2014, leading the Bulldogs to their first-ever No. 1 ranking.

Known for his dual-threat ability, he set multiple school records and earned All-SEC honors. Despite his college success, he was considered a mid-round selection in the NFL. Prescott was eventually drafted in the fourth round of the 2016 NFL Draft by the Dallas Cowboys.
